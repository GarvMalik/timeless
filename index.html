The details · 02

Quiet by design.

01 Peer to peer Video travels straight between browsers over WebRTC. No middle-man recording the room.
02 No accounts Nothing to install, no email, no password. A link is the only credential.
03 Call with friends A handful of people, one mesh — everyone connects straight to everyone else, no server in between.
04 Music & movies Stream a tab of music or a full movie with sound — everyone hears and watches the same thing, together.
05 Chat & names A lightweight text panel and a name on every tile, so it always reads like the right room.
06 Hardened Strict content-security-policy, self-hosted code, a host-gated waiting room. See SECURITY.md.
