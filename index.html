The details · 02

Quiet by design.

01 Peer to peer Video travels straight between browsers over WebRTC. No middle-man recording the room.
02 No accounts Nothing to install, no email, no password. A link is the only credential.
03 Share the screen Swap your camera for your desktop mid-call with a single tap, then swap back.
04 Runs anywhere A static page on GitHub Pages. It loads instantly and works on any modern browser.
